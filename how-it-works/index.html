  1. You choose 8 different numbers from 1-59 to make up a Lottery Bingo line.
  2. You register your lines with us (up to 15 of them) and we enter them into the next game for you.
  3. You set up a Standing Order to pay Â£1 per line, per week to {{ site.name }}. We'll email you the necessary bank details when you register.
  4. Using the National Lottery Lotto draw on a Saturday Night we'll mark numbers off your line(s). You can monitor them on this website.
  5. A winner or winners will be declared when a line has all 8 numbers marked off and the prize fund will be split between them.
  6. From the money collected 50% goes into the prize fund and 50% goes to {{ site.name }}.
